About

How It Works

Your Mount Rushmore is the four directors, writers, and actors who define your personal taste — scored by how much you've watched them, how highly you rated their work, and how far above your own average they rank.
The Scoring Formula
📈 Volume × Quality
Films seen × avg rating on a log scale — watching 10 films at 4★ beats 3 films at 5★, with diminishing returns on volume
65%
✨ Taste Affinity
How far above your personal rating average you score their films — makes your Rushmore uniquely yours
25%
🔁 Rewatches
Films you've logged more than once — a strong signal of genuine love
10%
Weights are normalized so the relative ratios always matter, even if they don't sum to 100%. Adjust them with the ⚖ Settings button. Minimum 3 films required to qualify.
Your Data
Your Letterboxd export is processed entirely in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server except the TMDB API for director/writer/actor lookups. Your API key is stored only in your browser's localStorage and never leaves your device.
Writers & Actors
Writers are credited for Screenplay, Story, and Adaptation roles. Actors are the top 5 billed cast members per film. Both use the same scoring formula as directors.
